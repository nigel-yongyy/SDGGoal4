Organisations

Here are some organisations that are working towards achieving SDG Goal 4

AIESEC (SPEAK UP)

AIESEC is aglobal platform for young people to develop their leadership potential through practical experiencesof many kinds, including internships, volunteering opportunities, and more.

Founded in 1948, AIESEC is a non-governmental and not-for-profit organization entirely run by youth for youth.

Save The Children

LSave the Children works in the United States and around the world to reach those children who are missing out most on learning and education.

We help children get ready for kindergarten and learn to read whihc is a major indicator of future success. We also ensure that no child’s learning stops because they are caught up in crisis.

World Education

We are education development experts who believe that everyone has the right to a high-quality education.Equity and inclusion drive our work.

Our work is driven by the belief that education is a powerful tool to improve the quality of life for people worldwide. Our programs creates equitable access to services that supports without discrimination.
